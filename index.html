EYE DOCTORS AND SURGEONS

 

Serving the communities of Traralgon, Gippsland and surrounding areas

At ValleyCare Eye Doctors, we are committed to providing high-quality eye consultations and treatment, using the latest technology and surgical techniques. Our team of Australian-trained eye specialists (ophthalmologists) are experienced in helping our patients achieve good eye health, in a supportive environment.

SERVICES

  • Cataract surgery
  • YAG/SLT lasers
  • Retinal laser
  • Age-related macular degeneration
  • Glaucoma treatment
  • Diabetic eye disease
  • Neuro-ophthalmology
  • Paediatric ophthalmology
  • Minor Oculoplastic procedures
